Landed Is Not Another Sales Coaching Tool

The core breakthrough

  • Sub-100ms AI inference inside live human conversations
  • Most “real-time” AI products still operate at 2-5 second latency
  • Landed can intervene while the conversation is still happening

Why it matters

The product is the infrastructure layer for high-pressure conversations.

  • Sales is the first wedge because ROI can be proven quickly
  • Contact centres are the larger market because compliance risk is massive
  • The same system applies anywhere humans speak under pressure

The investor framing

  • A sales coaching feature raises like a vertical SaaS tool
  • A real-time conversation intelligence platform raises like infrastructure
  • The company is built around latency, not generic coaching content

New framing: under-100ms live conversation AI · sales wedge · contact-centre platform

Why Gong Or Microsoft Cannot Copy This Overnight

Proprietary real-time architecture

  • Built for sub-100ms inference, not post-call summarisation
  • Optimised audio pipeline, model routing, and response timing
  • Designed to operate before the user loses the conversational moment

Data flywheel

Every live conversation improves the system.

  • Capture conversation patterns, interventions, and outcomes
  • Learn company-specific playbooks, objections, risks, and language
  • Switching costs grow as Landed becomes tuned to each team

Enterprise barriers

  • SOC 2, ISO 27001, FCA-adjacent workflows, and regulated deployment paths
  • Compliance-focused datasets are harder to acquire than generic sales calls
  • Incumbents are optimised for recording and analysis, not live intervention

Moat: architecture + live data + regulated certifications + customer-specific learning

  • Can justify £2k-£5k per month if Landed moves revenue outcomes

The Billion-Pound Path Runs Through Contact Centres

Why contact centres

  • 15m agents in the US and 4m in the UK
  • Typical software spend: £800-£1,500 per agent per year
  • 1% UK penetration implies £320m-£600m ARR potential

Why sub-100ms changes the category

Compliance value exists before the wrong sentence is spoken.

  • Flag FCA-sensitive language while the agent is still speaking
  • Prevent mis-selling, policy breaches, and regulatory exposure in real time
  • One avoided fine can justify enterprise-wide deployment

Milestones

  • Year 1: sales wedge, £500k ARR, measurable win-rate improvement
  • Year 2: regulated contact-centre pilots, £3m-£5m ARR
  • Year 3-4: compliance and performance layer, £20m-£50m ARR

Exit path: £50m ARR in regulated real-time conversation AI · strategic buyers at 20-30x revenue
